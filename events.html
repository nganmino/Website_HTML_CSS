Nordic Ideation Camp 2018

NIC is a one-week project camp for students from Finland, Denmark and Estonia to come together to design services and generate solution for the local businesses. At NIC, participants work in teams, join brainstorming sessions, attend meeting with the local business representatives, conduct a market research and come up with conclusions and suggestions for solutions.

There is professional support from the local business representatives, teachers from the three countries, and the campus staff where the NIC takes place.

There is also an event team to help make NIC more enjoyable and more fun.

Porvoo Alumni Event 2018

Porvoo Alumni Event is held at the campus Porvoo in April every year. The campus invite all alumni of the campus, along with commissioners and campus staff to come spend a lovely evening together.

The event aims to bring the networking opportunities for its attendees, inspire the current students from the stories of the successful alumnus, and to celebreate the hardwork of the campus staff that contribute to the work and study of the campus.

The event team undertake the tasks of planning, organizing, and serving during the event.

ATLAS Business Tourism SIG Conference 2019

ATLAS Special Interest Group Business Tourism work as a playground for researchers, teachers and practitioners in the field to gather, connect, share ideas, and together advance their knowledge of the field. Therefore, the priority mission of the ATLAS Business Tourism SIG Conference 2019 is to invite and host those targeted audience during a two-day conference in Porvoo.

In the program, most of the sessions are reserved for research paper presentations and other thought-provoking speeches from the guest speakers.
